Notes for New CS Students at Mtsac

  • Java is a better choice for a beginner, but it doesn't mean you should not explore C++.
    Try Java first, then you can see some simliarities between C++ and Java, so you can learn C++ easier.

  • I encourage every CS studnet to join Mtsac CS Club because it is a community for you to understand CS.
    Better still, you can find out CS events to particpate becuase CS resources are improtant to every CS students.

  • By the way, every CS student need to transfer because Mtsac doesn't offer you a CS degree.
    As a result, you need to plan your transfer as early as possible and take a lot of math, CS, and other STEAM related courses

  • Your activities should not be limited to only CS classes. You should seek for activities out of the Club as well.
    Try to apply for some research program related to science to improve your transfer application.

  • Try to take some programming courses part of CIS because CS department is belong to Math department and doesn't teach you technical skills.
    If you want to gain more experience with technical skills related to programming, take their classes.
